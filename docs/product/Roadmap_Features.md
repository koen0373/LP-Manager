# LiquiLab Product Roadmap

## Pricing & Plans (As of 2025-11-11)

- Prices scale in bundles of 5 pools.
- VISITOR — Free. Public info, demos.
- PREMIUM — $14.95/mo incl. 5 pools; +$9.95 per extra 5. RangeBand™ and per-pool detail pages.
- PRO — $24.95/mo incl. 5 pools; +$14.95 per extra 5. Includes analytics.
- ENTERPRISE — On request (multi-wallet, API, SLA).
- Alerts add-on — +$2.49/mo per 5 pools (RangeBand™ Alerts).
- Trials — 14 days on Premium and Pro. Monthly cancel.
**Examples:** Premium 5 pools no alerts: $14.95 • Premium 10 pools + alerts: $29.88 • Pro 15 pools + alerts: $62.32  
_Source: `/config/pricing.json` and `/api/public/pricing`._
